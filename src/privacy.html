Privacy Policy

Mission

In the spirit of transparency and general good faith, this site's privacy policy strives to be simple and easily understood.

What we collect

This site is served on Amazon Web Services' cloud platform and leverages the CloudFront CDN. All optional tracking, logging, and reporting is disabled. The infrastructure setup is open source and available in our repository.

The following information is automatically collected by CloudFront
  • Type of the device (e.g. desktop, mobile)
  • Browser (e.g. Chrome, Firefox)
  • Operation system (e.g. Linux, macOS, Windows)
  • Locations, by country or by U.S. state/territory
For additional information, please see CloudFront's viewer reports documentation

How we use gathered information

In short, we do not. We use the Viewer Reports component of CloudFront to check how many requests we receive to forecast billing costs.

How we store and protect information

Amazon Web Services encrypts data in transit and at rest. This site employs HTTPS and SSL for its part. For further information, please see CloudFront's Data Protection documentation

With whom do we share gathered information

We do not share gathered information.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies. We do not track users.

Do Not Sell My Information

We do not sell anything, including user's information.
